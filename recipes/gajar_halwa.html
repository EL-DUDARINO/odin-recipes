RECIPE FOR GAJAR KA HALWA


DESCRIPTION

Gajar ka halwa, also known as gajorer halua, gajrela, gajar pak, and carrot halwa is a carrot-based sweet dessert pudding made by placing grated carrots in a pot containing a specific amount of water, milk and sugar, cardamom and then cooking while stirring regularly. It is often served with a garnish of almonds and pistachios.

Gajar ka halwa originally contained carrots, milk and ghee but nowadays includes many other ingredients like mava (khoya). Being a combination of milk and carrots it is known as milk flavored gajar ka halwa but in the other case, the combination of cream or mava (khoya) and carrot is described as mava flavored gajar ka halwa.

INGREDIENTS REQUIRED

  • CARROTS
  • SUGAR
  • MILK
  • BUTTER
  • PISTACHIOS
  • ALMONDS
  • RAISINS
  • CASHEW NUTS

STEPS FOR COOKING

  1. Wash, peel and grate carrots in a container.
  2. Add the grated carrots to a cooker with a little amount of water and put them to boil.
  3. Now, add milk to a cooker and let it boil and don't forget to stir it slowly the whole time.
  4. Boil the milk till it turns into a paste and take it out in a container.
  5. Now, put the boiled carrots in a pan on medium flame and let it cook for a while. Basically dry them out.
  6. As, they start to dry out add sugar as per your liking and the paste we made earlier and mix it thoroughly.
  7. Now, wait for the mixture to dry out.
  8. The dish is ready but just before putting out the flame, add the dry fruits and mix for the last time.
  9. Your dish is ready to serve.
