RECIPE FOR DAL CHAWAL



DESCRIPTION

Dal Chawal is an Indian dish. It is one of the easiest and most tasty dish that anyone can cook. It has different varieties. Dal in dal chawal means a kind of curry made up of lentils. As we know there are different types of lentils out there. However, the recipe is common for them all.

Dal Chawal have excellent nutritional value, be it protein or fibre. The main recipe given here is for DAL only, as chawal is nothing but BOILED RICE, we suppose anyone can cook. So, without further ado lets get into cooking:

INGREDIENTS REQUIRED:

  • WHITE RICE
  • LENTIL(OF YOUR CHOICE)
  • SALT
  • RED PEPPER
  • BLACK PEPPER
  • ONION
  • COOKING OIL
  • CUMIN SEEDS

STEPS FOR COOKING:

  1. Boil Rice in a pressure cooker and keep them aside, they are done.
  2. Now, Boil lentil in another pressure cooker.
  3. Heat cooking oil in a pan and add cumin seeds and finely chopped onion until brown.
  4. Add salt, black pepper, red pepper as per your taste.
  5. Now add the boiled lentils and cook for a minute.
  6. Your dish is ready. Serve with some pickle and raw onions for best taste.
